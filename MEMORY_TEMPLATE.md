## What to Include
- **Facts only**: What happened, what was decided, what needs doing
- **Status changes**: App submissions, reviews, launches
- **Deadlines**: Review dates, testing completion dates
- **Action items**: What needs to be checked or done next
- **Islamic terms**: Use insha'Allah (if God wills), masha'Allah (what God wills), barak Allahu feek (blessings) sparingly

## What to Exclude
- Greetings and salutations
- Emojis and decorative elements
- Personal feelings or commentary
- Repetitive information
- Narrative descriptions
- "Thoughts" sections
- Excessive religious terms - keep them minimal and natural
